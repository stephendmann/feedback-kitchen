### Section 1 — Architecture

**Sound at a structural level with gaps at every boundary.**

The `brand-film.html` as iframe target is a valid isolation pattern — it allows the film to be updated without touching the wrapper page. The risks:

1. **Hosting gap (CRITICAL):** No `.mp4`/`.webm` exists. If `brand-film.html` ships with a placeholder `src`, the `<video>` renders a broken element. Users see: native video error icon, or empty space. No fallback is defined.

2. **End card CTA (CRITICAL):** Links inside `brand-film.html` navigate the iframe's browsing context by default. The "Try the demo scorer" CTA at Scene 9 will open inside the iframe — breaking the payoff action. All links must carry `target="_top"`.

3. **CSP/Sandbox (HIGH):** If `how-to-feedback-kitchen.html` sets a Content-Security-Policy header, `<iframe>` may need `allow-scripts` and `allow-same-origin` in a `sandbox` attribute. Without the sandbox, the CSP on the parent page might block iframe execution depending on hosting setup.

4. **Fullscreen (MEDIUM):** Native fullscreen from a `<video>` inside an iframe requires `allow="fullscreen"` on the `<iframe>` element. Without it, the fullscreen button in native video controls does nothing.
