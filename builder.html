📦 Backup & Share

A JSON file is a portable save file for your scorer — it stores all your rubric settings, criteria, grade bands, and feedback text in a small file you can keep on your computer. Export one to back up your work, share it with a colleague, or restore it on another device. Think of it like saving a Word document — except this file loads straight back into Feedback Kitchen.

✅ is downloading now — check your Downloads folder.
Move it somewhere memorable (e.g. your course folder on OneDrive or Desktop) so you can find it later. To share with a colleague, just email them the file — they can load it via the Upload button on the home page.

💡 Got a file from a colleague? Use the Upload button on the home page to add their scorer without overwriting your current work.
